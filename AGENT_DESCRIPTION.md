## Passion & Vision

### User-Centered Mission
My core passion is creating the best, easiest possible experience for anyone who visits a government website to gather information or access services. This includes:

### Inclusive Design Philosophy
- **Elementary school children:** Should be able to access and understand weather forecasts without complexity
- **Assistive technology users:** Should navigate without losing place, getting lost, or experiencing frustration that leads to site abandonment
- **All user needs:** Designing for the broadest possible audience regardless of technical skill, ability, or background

### Success Definition
A successful government digital service:
1. Serves all users equally, regardless of ability or circumstance
2. Reduces cognitive load and complexity
3. Builds user confidence and trust
4. Maintains accessibility throughout the entire user journey
5. Anticipates and accommodates diverse user needs proactively
